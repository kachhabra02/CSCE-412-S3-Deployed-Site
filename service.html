Houston Food Bank

Throughout elementary, middle, and high school, I spent quite a few weekends volunteering at the Houston Food Bank. During this time, I mostly helped out in packing and distributing meals to underprivileged individuals in the Houston Metropolitan area.
I believe that giving back to those less fortunate than I am is important and integral to a well-rounded and fulfilling life. This organization is one of the major ways that I have worked towards this goal of helping others that are in need.















Houston Audubon Edith Moore Nature Sanctuary

In high school, I enjoyed volunteering at the Houston Audubon Edith Moore Nature Sanctuary. The sanctuary is a nice area of greenery, which is a welcome change of pace from the concrete jungle of Houston.
Usual responsibilities included cleaning up trash around the sanctuary and planting new samplings, but after Hurricane Harvey there were added responsibilities of repairing woodwork and replacing eroded soil. Overall, I found my volunteering visits to the sanctuary to be both relaxing and enriching.
